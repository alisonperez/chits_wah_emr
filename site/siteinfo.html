About CHITS

CHITS stands for Community Health Information Tracking System. This is an open-source initiative funded by PANASIA-ICT and is a collaborative project among the following partners:

  1. Pasay City Local Government Unit Health Office
  2. UP College of Medicine MIU
  3. UP College of Medicine COME
  4. SafeKids International
  5. DOST-ASTI

All access to this site is monitored and can be audited at any time. Privacy violations and attempts to breach site security will be immediately reported to proper authorities for prosecution under the Philippine Electronic Commerce Act.
